Part of the global economy, global payments are a key part of the financial transaction network known as the Global Payments System. Bitcoin was created to address some of the major challenges facing the Global Payments System. Global Payments is a U.S.-based financial consultancy focused on improving the global payments system, particularly in the implementation of digital currencies and blockchain technology. In a recent interview, Global Payments CEO Richard Tulloch said the company plans to work more closely with major UK financial institutions. Says Tulloch, "We're starting to talk to banks and businesses about how blockchain is a disruptive technology and how it can help them improve their business." The company is also working closely with the BBA (British Bankers Association) to help banks understand the potential of blockchain technology.
